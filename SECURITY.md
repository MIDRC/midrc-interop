# Reporting Vulnerabilities
---
If you discover a vulnerability in this project, please report it privately via email at midrc-support@gen3.org.
Alternatively, use our secure web form at https://www.midrc.org/midrc-contact.

# Disclosure Policy
---
We aim to disclose fixes for reported vulnerabilities within 30 days of receiving a report.
We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 1-3 business days.

# Practice
---
All vulnerabilities are handled confidentially to protect users until a patch is ready.
Please do not publicly disclose any vulnerability prior to our coordinated disclosure process.
